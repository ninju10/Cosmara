Our Neighbourhood

Featured Planets

Four worlds that define our solar neighbourhood — each a story of extremes.

Earth

Our Home

The only known harbour of life. A pale blue dot of liquid oceans, breathable skies, and 8 billion stories unfolding each second.

Mars

The Red Planet

Rust-red and haunting. Olympus Mons towers three times the height of Everest. Our next civilisational outpost — dusty, frozen, and waiting.

Distance from Sun 1.52 AU

Jupiter

The Giant

A world so vast it could swallow all other planets combined. Its Great Red Spot — a storm larger than Earth — has raged for 350 years.

Saturn

Lord of the Rings

Our Journey

Mission Timeline

Six decades of courage, ingenuity, and relentless curiosity.

🛰
1957

First Satellite

Sputnik 1 broke the silence of space with a single radio beep — and changed history forever. The space age had begun.

🌕
1969

Moon Landing

Apollo 11 landed on the Sea of Tranquility. Neil Armstrong's first step was watched by 600 million people — a fifth of humanity.

🤖
2004

Mars Rovers

Spirit and Opportunity landed on Mars. Designed for 90 days, Opportunity kept transmitting for 14 years — a love letter to persistence.

🏠
2040+

Mars Colony

The first permanent off-world human settlement — a self-sustaining city under the Martian sky. A second cradle for civilisation.
